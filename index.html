About Gert

Meet a curious little loggerhead with a heart for discovery

Gert is a young sea turtle inspired by the South Carolina coast. With kindness, curiosity, and a sense of wonder, she explores the shoreline and introduces children to the fun side of learning.

SEE stands for Science · Education · Exploration

That is the whole point of the series: make learning feel natural, memorable, and light. No heavy-handed lecture. Just smart storytelling that does not talk down to kids.

Meet the author

Created by Maxine Moody Poston

Inspired by the beauty of the South Carolina coast, Maxine Moody Poston created Gert to help young readers explore the world through imagination, kindness, and curiosity.

A coastal voice with a classroom-friendly mission

The Gert series blends storytelling and education in a way that feels warm, visual, and easy for families to enjoy together. It is built for read-aloud moments, early learning reinforcement, and the kind of books kids ask for again instead of quietly abandoning on a shelf.

FAQ

Quick answers for parents, readers, and curious turtles

What age range is the series best for?

The books are designed for young children and early readers, especially families looking for bright educational read-aloud stories.

What does “SEE” mean?

SEE stands for Science, Education, and Exploration, which is the learning framework that shapes the series.

Can I preview the books?

Yes. Each featured title includes a downloadable PDF so visitors can sample the story and artwork.
