About Gert

Meet a curious loggerhead with a love for discovery

Gert is a curious loggerhead sea turtle who lives along the South Carolina coast. Rather than swimming away with her siblings, she stays near the beach to explore, observe, and learn.

SEE stands for Science, Education, and Exploration

That idea sits at the heart of the series: children are invited to notice the world around them, ask questions, and enjoy learning through warm, playful stories.

Meet the Author

Created by Maxine Moody Poston

Inspired by the South Carolina coast and a lifelong love of storytelling, Maxine created the Gert books to help children learn to read, count, recognize colors, understand emotions, and stay curious about the world.

A warm, educational approach to story time

The series was originally created to help her own children learn, and it continues that same mission: combining imagination, coastal adventure, and early learning into books that are welcoming, playful, and memorable.

Whether the story is about counting, colors, or feelings, every book is designed to support read-aloud moments, encourage conversation, and make learning feel natural.

FAQ

Quick answers for parents, readers, and classrooms

What age range is the series best for?

The books are a strong fit for young children, early readers, and families looking for engaging read-aloud stories.

What does SEE mean?

SEE stands for Science, Education, and Exploration, the learning framework that guides the series.

Can I preview the books online?

Yes. Each featured title includes a PDF link so visitors can open a book and explore the artwork and story.
